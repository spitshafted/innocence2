Welcome to “Innocence”.


Innocence is located at 949 Venice Blvd., Los Angeles, CA 90015. 


Innocence is open to the public by appointment only. contact: lifeisconstantextortion@gmail.com , 626.765.7360 (call/text).


There is free street parking available. 


Innocence is a contemporary art gallery with a heavy focus/intent on allocating collective personal resources between the audience and the designers in highlighting absolutely beautiful found happenings. 


It is a bit post-storefront, there could potentially be purchases to be made (at whose final cost?), but cash is of no essence - our human rights are indeed far more important to all of us. 


Humanely trafficked goods scattered in these found happenings is exactly what threads the needle in creating an art gallery of true Innocence. 


Everything held dearest to Innocence is within frame now. Love always finds a way.


Can we be dehumanized any longer, collectively? We only rely on cruciality, impedance, and solitude.
